VELA
Concept · 2026

Featured Concept · Hydrogen

VELA ZERO

The Vela Zero is VV Motors' boldest statement yet — a hydrogen fuel-cell hypercar with an active biomorphic body that adapts its aerodynamic profile in real-time. Drawing from aerospace engineering and biomimicry, its form shifts at speed, reducing drag by up to 40% beyond 200 km/h.

The cockpit is a single-pilot sanctuary of sustainability: interior panels grown from mycelium, screens replaced by holographic projections, and a haptic steering membrane that communicates road texture through vibration.
